We Are Our World

Our World is an organisation based around enviormental protection. We hope to spread awareness and to educate people so that we can all start to change for the better.


Our current office is situated in England, London. We've ensured that the building is environmentally friendly. The building is powered solely by 3 windmills on the roof of the facility. We plan on opening more facilties around the globe with hopes of employing people who are as passionate as we are.

Our offices - located in Canary Wharf, London.
